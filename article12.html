Avenue in five-goal frenzy

By Derrick Lynch

Avenue United cruised into the semi-final of the Clare Cup with a resounding 5-0 victory over Kildysart Celtic in Roslevan last Sunday.

Simon Cuddy opened the scoring soon after the game began with a gorgeous strike and from there on, it was plain sailing for the home side as they coasted past their rivals.

Soon after, Dylan Casey set Mark Roche free with a fine though ball and Roche slotted it home to establish a commanding lead for Avenue with only six minutes played.

Avenue were throwing the ball around with ease and dictated the play easily and, in the 25th minute, Alan Roche played it to Mikey Mahoney after a lovely team move and O’Mahoney all but ended the tie as a contest.

Once the third goal went in, the game slowed considerably and Avenue rested on their laurels to take them to the break 3-0 up.

It was more of the same in the second half and when Conor Mullen grabbed his two goals, the game had long been over in earnest.

It was an impressive display from Avenue, as they coasted into the final four of the competition.
