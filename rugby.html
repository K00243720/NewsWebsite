Ennis girls hang tough for Munster finals

A huge weekend lies in store for Ennis Rugby Club, as the U-18 girls go in search of Munster glory.

Women powering through cup competition

Ennis women made it two from two with a comprehensive win over UL Bohs on Saturday afternoon in Fr McNamara Park. A nip and tuck first half saw just a try separated the sides at half-time. However, the second half was dominated by the home side, who ran out deserving winners on what was an otherwise tough afternoon.
