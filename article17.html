Home side outgunned in Paddy's Day encounter

By Derrick Lynch

The first round of the Division 1 Camogie League between Scariff-Ogonnelloe and St Joseph’s was played in St Joseph’s at 12 noon on St Patrick’s Day.

In tough windy conditions this game proved to be a hard-fought battle for both sides. St Joseph’s had the wind in the first half and opened the scoring with a point however a great work-rate from the Scariff-Ogonnelloe backs led to two points up front, one of which was a free from Mairead Scanlon. .

The tough weather conditions kept scoring low with only two more points coming in the opening half - one for each of the sides. This left the half-time score at 0-3 points to 0-2 in favour of Scariff-Ogonnelloe.

With the wind at their backs in the second half, Scariff-Ogonnelloe pushed on opening the half with a well needed goal. St Joseph’s battled hard and within 10 minutes raised a green flag of their own putting only a point between the sides again. Scariff-Ogonnelloe added three more points from Mairead Scanlon, Lynda Daly and Aisling Corbett to finish with a tally of 1-6.

Meanwhile, St Joseph’s added a point to their goal leaving them with 1-3.

Scariff-Ogonnelloe: Rachael Daly; Holly McGee, Susan Daly, Aoife Maloney; Alison Madden, Amy Barrett, Alison McGee; Mairead Scanlon, Lynda Daly; Roisin O’Brien, Aisling Corbett, Aisling Corry; Danielle Sheedy, Aoife Power, Jennifer Daly. Subs: Laura Brody for Danielle Sheedy, Clodagh Nash for Roisin O’Brien.
